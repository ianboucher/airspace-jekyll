A LITTLE BACKGROUND...

Once upon a time, I was a Formula 1 engineer. But now I'm not. "Why would anyone want to leave F1?", you ask. Well for me, the best part of my work was writing (bad) code to solve engineering problems, so I decided it was time to learn how to do it properly. Fast-forward a while and here I am - a freshly minted full-stack web developer. Now I live in south Devon near the sea, where I wake early at home (no more hotel rooms), make coffee and sit down to write some code.

I LOVE BUILDING THINGS

The best thing about web development is building beautifully crafted applications that solve real problems and that people love to use.

Some of my own projects include a full-featured, mobile responsive, Spotify-esque music player, an art marketplace featuring with Stripe integration and inline image processing, and an educational game platform built with Angular and PHP/Laravel.

I love to use my unusual cross-section of tradional engineering and problem solving skills, forged in the high-pressure environment of motorpsort, in combination with programming skills to take a fresh approach to designing and building great applications.
